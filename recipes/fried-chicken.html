Fried Chicken

Description

Crispiest and most delicious fried chicken recipe.

Original recipe

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/8805/crispy-fried-chicken/

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cut up the chicken into pieces.
  2. Put flour in a plastic bag and season it with paprika, salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Dip the chicken pieces in buttermilk then put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.
  4. Place the coated chicken in a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper. Let it sit until the flour is of paste-like consistency.
  5. Fill a large skillet about ⅓ to ½ full with vegetable oil. Heat until very hot.
  6. Put in as many chicken pieces as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in hot oil on both sides.
  7. When browned, reduce heat and cover skillet; let cook for 30 minutes (the chicken will be cooked through but not crispy). Remove cover, raise heat again, and continue to fry until crispy.
  8. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels. Depending on how much chicken you have, you may have to fry in a few shifts. Keep the finished chicken in a slightly warm oven while preparing the rest.
